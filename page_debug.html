Background

Under the Project No. 20 - Innovative Extension Approaches for Revitalizing Agriculture in J&K - Kisan Khidmat Ghar (KKG) as has been envisioned as a “One Stop Extension Service Centre” at panchayat level to extend smart technology driven seamless agri-extension services.

Functions Of Kisan Khidmat Ghar Portal

Key Objectives

    To act as one-stop service center for agriculture and allied services at panchayat level.
    To extend all schemes & services by agriculture & allied department to farmer’s doorstep.
    To ensure timely availability of quality agri-inputs to farmers at predefined prices.

Comprehensive Farmer Registration and Support System

Farmers will undergo a streamlined registration process for schemes and services like HADP & CSS, establishing unique profiles inclusive of farm details and documentation.

Integrated Farming Support and Market Access

Facilitates the seamless sale of farm inputs such as seeds, fertilizers, and pesticides at the grassroots level through established market linkages.

Integrated Advisory and Resource Access

Provides comprehensive advisory services encompassing static guides on crop, disease, and post-harvest management, alongside real-time updates on weather forecasting, agronomic advice, and market intelligence.
